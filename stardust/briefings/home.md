# Intent

The new Modelic homepage should make a decision-maker at a brand feel they're standing in a quietly confident workshop where the people behind the work are visible and the work itself is the headline. It signals — without fanfare — that Modelic is a tastemaker shop, not a retainer mill: design and engineering done well, with a modern toolkit (including AI) used where it earns its place. By the end of the page, the only natural next step is to start a project conversation.

# Audience

Decision-makers at brands evaluating an agency for a real project — VPs of Marketing, e-comm leads, brand directors, founders. They've usually already seen 3–5 agency sites and are scanning for evidence that this one ships work like theirs at a level they recognise. They want proof, not promises.

Returning visitors and design talent are explicitly **not** the homepage's job. Returning prospects route to `/contact`; talent routes to `/careers` from the footer.

# Key Messages

1. **The work is the proof.** Brands like yours have shipped commerce, accessibility, and brand work with us — see it before you read another claim.
2. **Outcomes, not retainers.** Modelic is built around measurable value (conversion, performance, cost), not perpetual maintenance billing.
3. **Modern toolkit, used like grown-ups.** Modern CMS, modern commerce, AI applied where it earns its place — never as the headline, never as a category claim.
4. **Partner, not vendor.** Modelic positions itself inside the client's team. The "vending machine" register stays — but the *line itself* lives on the Approach page, not here.
5. **Real people behind the work.** A glimpse of the team — enough warmth to differentiate from a faceless agency, not enough to clutter the proof.

# Calls to Action

- **Primary:** "Let's talk about your project" → `/contact`. Anchored in the closing CTA block; phone (801-363-0101) and email visible.
- **Secondary:** "See all work" → `/work`. After the Selected Work section.
- **Tertiary:** "How we work" → `/approach` (or longest service page that hosts the full tenets). After the Approach (distilled) block.

# Tone

Quietly confident. Plain-spoken, partner-minded, slightly self-effacing — but the homepage is the *least* self-deprecating page on the site. Save the "we are not a vending machine" line for the Approach page.

The page is calm and ordered, work-led. AI/modern-stack mentions are subtle and singular: one line in the hero, one tenet in the approach block, optionally one tag on a case-study tile. Three quiet drops, never a banner.

**Forbidden phrases:** AI-powered · world-class · best-in-class · industry-leading · transformative · synergy · leverage · unlock · explosive · cutting-edge.

**Numbers are welcome.** The "measurable value" claim has to cash somewhere — outcome stats (uplift %, conversion lift, build cost reduction) belong in the Selected Work and the closing block.

**Motion is part of the tone.** Per `.impeccable.md`, the homepage is licensed to be expressive in motion: the wave mark is kinetic by default, hero entry can be theatrical, hover states can have personality. Subtlety is not the goal — *specificity* is.
