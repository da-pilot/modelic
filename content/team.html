// 03 / Working with us
How a project actually works.

A typical engagement runs 8–14 weeks for a focused build, longer for a partnership relationship. Every project starts with a kickoff that names the metric we're moving — together — and ends with a handoff that leaves your internal team able to extend the work without us.

Weekly demos, shared documents in your tools, decisions captured in plain language. We don't run two parallel agency processes — yours and ours. We adopt yours where it's good and bring our own only where there's a gap.

If a project goes wrong, we say so on the next call. If we're going to miss a date, we say it the moment we know. That's the cornerstone.
