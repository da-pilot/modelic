// 02 / Perspective
What we hold to in design work.

Design that ships looks effortless. Most of that effort is in saying no to the third option that would have made the second option harder to maintain. We design for the team that has to extend the work after we leave — which is usually a smaller team than the one we worked with on the project.

The constraint is the brief. Type and color and rhythm are the answer.

We use AI as a research and ideation accelerator — not as a generation shortcut. Real photography, real type, real craft survives a refresh in five years. AI-generated visual assets do not. The toolkit serves the craft; the craft is the brand.

If a moodboard could be assembled by anyone, it isn't a moodboard. It's a search query.

// 03 / What we ship
What shipping design with Modelic looks like.

Brand systems

Identity, type, color, voice. The system you'll extend in five years — not just the launch deck.

UX research

User interviews, audit reads, decision frameworks. We bring the question; the data tells us the answer.

Interaction + visual design

From wireframes to high-fidelity prototypes. Iterated in the browser, not in Figma. The thing you see is the thing you ship.

Design systems

Token sets, component libraries, documentation that stays current. We hand off something your team will extend — not something they'll work around.

Design QA

From dev-handoff through launch. The QA pass that catches the misaligned 4px before someone tweets a screenshot.

Brand evolution

Refresh, not rebuild. The bones that make your brand recognisable today are usually the right bones — even if everything around them needs to change.

// 04 / Modern toolkit
Where AI earns its place in design work.
Reference gathering and competitive landscape audits. Voice-and-tone exploration where we need 30 variants to find the one that lands. Stock-photo replacement when commissioning isn't in scope and the alternative is a generic stock site. We use AI to widen the funnel of candidate ideas — never to replace the judgment that picks one. The output is always real craft.
// 05 / Partnership
